About

A creative technologist at heart, Arvin Leeuwis translates ideas and brands into digital experiences. After several years of working as a creative developer, Arvin now works as a freelance creative technologist—creating animations, websites and motion graphics for various purposes. His work is often culture-driven, aimed to amplify stories, products and experiences in a meaningful way.

Arvin has worked with numerous brands and organisations, including Zes Nullen, Feels Agency, Fabienne Chapot, Amsterdam Vintage Watches, Cobo Soles and the Christopher Wallace Foundation.
